Support Reforestation Efforts with Nyalil Tree Nursery

Baringo, Kenya, is facing significant environmental challenges due to deforestation. The Nyalil Tree Nursery aims to restore the land, promote biodiversity, and empower the local community. Your support is crucial to achieving these goals.

Environmental Challenges in Baringo, Kenya

Deforestation and environmental degradation in Baringo have led to severe impacts:

  • Loss of Biodiversity: Native plant and animal species are disappearing due to habitat loss.
  • Soil Erosion: The lack of tree cover leads to soil erosion, reducing land fertility.
  • Climate Change Impact: Deforestation exacerbates climate change, leading to unpredictable weather patterns.
  • Water Scarcity: Deforestation disrupts the water cycle, leading to reduced water availability.
  • Loss of Livelihoods: Local communities reliant on natural resources face economic hardship.
  • Desertification: The land is turning into desert due to the loss of vegetation cover.
  • Food Insecurity: Agricultural productivity declines, leading to food shortages.

Our Mission: The Nyalil Tree Nursery aims to address these challenges by restoring the environment and empowering the community.
